SpaceX Rocket Fleet

Falcon 9

The Falcon 9 is a reusable, two-stage rocket designed and manufactured by SpaceX for the reliable and safe transport of people and payloads into Earth orbit and beyond.

First flight: June 4, 2010

Height: 70 m (229.6 ft)

Falcon Heavy

Falcon Heavy is the most powerful operational rocket in the world by a factor of two. With the ability to lift into orbit nearly 64 metric tons (141,000 lb).

First flight: February 6, 2018

Height: 70 m (229.6 ft)

Starship

Starship is a fully reusable, super heavy-lift launch vehicle under development by SpaceX, designed to carry both crew and cargo to Earth orbit, the Moon, Mars, and other destinations.

First flight: In development

Height: 120 m (394 ft)
